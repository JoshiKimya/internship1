About Us

IT BEGINNED SIMPLY ENOUGH LIKE THIS....

To Experience True Taste

Fakt Marathi  is a quick service restaurant that offers Vegetarian Maharashtrian fare in a traditional but unique setting. We offer traditional fare that has been hand-selected from around Maharashtra. The food we offer is made in a traditional manner using a blend of traditional Maharashtrian masala that is pleasing to Indian palates throughout the country. In order for our clients to enjoy the cuisine every day, it is rich in flavour and devoid of colouring, additives, and preservatives on our menu. Nothing is deep frozen and everything is prepared each day in a hygienic manner. As a result, it is light on the stomach as well as the pocket.
